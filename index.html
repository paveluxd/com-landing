How it plays

  1. Players get three units each.
  2. Move units towards the King’s Chamber.
  3. Play cards to get an advantage.
  4. When one player moves all units to the King’s Chamber, the game ends.
  5. Player with the most points wins.

Card Chain System

  • Players place cards at the start exposing their intent.
  • Cads activate when pushed into the 3rd link by other cards placed at the start.
  • Some cards can move the chain to the other side, activating cards from previous turns.
  • Player can avoid adding cards to the chain, to prevent the opponent from getting the card they wanted.

About

We played some old board games and decided to create our own game. Something simple and easy to pick up. After a few months of design and testing, we came up with our game concept.

We started a discord community and created a web prototype to play the game with every other day.

Now we plan to make the game more accessible. We want to improve our browser game and maybe release a physical version as well.

We look for a web developer to improve the game.
