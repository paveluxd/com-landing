How it plays

  1. Players get three units each.
  2. Move units towards the King’s area.
  3. Play cards to get an advantage.
  4. Once all units of one player reach
    King’s area, the game ends.
  5. Player with the most points wins.

Card Chain System

  • Players place cards at the start of the chain, exposing their intent.
  • Cads activate when pushed into the 3rd link.
  • Some cards can move the chain in reverse, activating cards from previous turns.
  • Player can avoid adding cards to the chain, to prevent the opponent from getting the card they wanted.

About

We played some old board games and decided to create our own. Something simple and quick. After a few months of designing and testing, we came up with our game concept.

We started a discord community and created a web prototype to play the game every other day.

Now we plan to make the game more accessible. We want to improve our browser game and someday release a physical version.

We look for a developer to improve the game.
